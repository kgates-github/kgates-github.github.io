The role of design on a product team is to drive the overall design process, share research responsibilities with the PM, lead co-creation, rapid prototyping, usability testing and validation, and to foster collaboration between design and engineering.

What is your approach to design?
My approach to design is to start with understanding the customer problems, then prioritize problems and brainstorm activities. I facilitate workshops and design sprints, and create product vision storyboards. I also use rapid prototyping to quickly get ideas out of our heads and into the world, and use weekly engineering reviews to get an early gut-check for complexity.
